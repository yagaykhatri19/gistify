What is Gistify?

Imagine you're holding a book in one hand and a magic wand in the other. With a flick of your wrist, you can transform that book into a concise, crystal-clear summary, capturing all the essential points without losing the essence. Sounds like magic, right? Well, that's exactly what Gistify does - but with text!

Meet Gistify: Your Text-Summarizing Wizard

Gistify is your go-to digital companion for turning lengthy texts into bite-sized, digestible nuggets of information. Whether you're a student drowning in textbooks, a professional swamped with reports, or just someone who loves to stay informed without spending hours reading, Gistify is here to help.

How Does Gistify Work?

At the heart of Gistify are two magical spells:

  • Abstractive Summary: This is Gistify's creative side. It reads your text, understands the core ideas, and then writes a brand-new summary in its own words. Think of it as having a conversation with a knowledgeable friend who explains things to you in a clear and engaging way.
  • Extractive Summary: This is Gistify's analytical side. It meticulously scans your text, identifies the most important sentences, and strings them together to form a concise summary. It's like having a highlighter that picks out only the most crucial points.
